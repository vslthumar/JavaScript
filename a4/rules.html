Game Rules

There are two dice to roll for each players.Roll the dice and put them in order to make the highest number possible. If you roll a 4 and an 6, for example, your best answer would be 64. It's a 2 players and 3 rounds game. Whoever among two players have the highest number possible wins the round. Round ties if both the players have same score. Whoever among two players wins most of the rounds is a winner. Game ties if both the players win 1-1 round and 1 round ties.
